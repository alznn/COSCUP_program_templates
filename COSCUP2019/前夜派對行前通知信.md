## How to get the badge of the conference? / 如何報到？

Please find your QRCode in the attachement. This is the universial ticket for the Welcome Party, the conference, and the coupon to get your T-shirt.

Also, please download our open-source App “OPass”. There are various things you can do with the app, you can even save your ticket in the app for fast-check-in procedure. 

The App for iOS and Android are at the final review stage, please search for "Opass" after Monday on Google Play Store or AppStore)

（請將附件的 QRCode 存下來，這就是專屬於您的前夜派對 + COSCUP + 講者 T-shirt 聯票。當然也歡迎您下載社群成員通力製作的 OPass 來管理您的電子票、取得大會更多資訊等等。新版 App 將於下週上線，歡迎屆時搜尋下載。）
